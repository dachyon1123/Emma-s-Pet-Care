What I Do

As a college student passionate about helping the dog community, I offer a caring and practical dog washing service tailored for busy pet owners. While I'm not a licensed groomer, I specialize in maintaining your dog’s cleanliness and comfort, especially when you're waiting for a professional haircut appointment. I provide convenient pickup and drop-off services to accommodate your schedule, ensuring a hassle-free experience for both you and your pet. Let me help keep your furry friend happy and clean!
