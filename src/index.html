What I Do

As a dedicated college student with a passion for helping the dog community, I offer support to pet owners in need of extra assistance with their dogs. While I am not a licensed groomer, my goal is to provide interim maintenance for your dog, ensuring they are well-cared for while awaiting their next professional grooming session. I understand the importance of regular care and attention for your furry friends and strive to make this period as comfortable as possible for both you and your pet. By entrusting me with your dog's care, you can be assured they are in good hands until their next haircut.

About Me

Hello there! With a big heart for both learning and giving back to my community, my passion lies in helping dogs. I'm constantly learning about their hair textures, grooming techniques, and overall maintenance. While I'm not licensed yet, my goal is to provide interim maintenance for your dog, ensuring they are well-cared for while awaiting their next professional grooming session. I understand the importance of regular care and attention for your furry friends and strive to make this period as comfortable as possible for both you and your pet. By entrusting me with your dog's care, you can be assured they are in good hands until their next haircut. Join me on this journey of compassion and skill development!

Do you offer nail trimmings?

I do not offer nail trimmings at the moment. This is something I will offer in the future.

Do you ever take my dogs off premises for any reason?

Your dog will never leave the premesis of the wash unless I am picking up and dropping off. When picking up and dropping off, I take them straight from the pick-up location straight to the wash, and then straight from the wash to the requested drop-off locaiton.

If my dog is reactive, will you still bathe him?

Due to liability reasons I do not offer baths to dogs that are reactive or aggresive.

Will my dog be around other dogs?

No, your dog will be washed by themselves for the entire duration of the wash
